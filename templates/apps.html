There are a number of ways to make it a little easier to use MediaCrush. Here are the official ways:

Web Browsers

We've made a Chrome extension and a Firefox add-on that lets you upload images on the web straight to MediaCrush. Give them a try!

Mobile

Using MediaCrush on the go is as simple as can be — just head over to https://mediacru.sh from your mobile device to use our mobile site.

Windows

Using Windows? the official MediaCrush Windows application. You can upload files straight from your desktop and send screenshots directly to MediaCrush. And of course, it's open source.

Linux

For Linux users, we have a nice bash script for uploading media to MediaCrush. Simple to use: mc file1.png file2.mp4 file3.mp3.

Everyone Else

We're still working on more ways to use MediaCrush on your system. Want it faster? Have a look at our straightforward API and see if you can make your own!
